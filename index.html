Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school timings? expand_more
Our standard school hours are from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM for Play Group and Nursery, and 9:00 AM to 1:30 PM for Junior and Senior KG. We also offer extended daycare facilities.
Do you provide transportation? expand_more
Yes, we provide GPS-enabled transportation within a 5km radius of the campus. Each van is accompanied by a female attendant for safety.
What is the teacher-child ratio? expand_more
We maintain a 1:10 ratio for Nursery and KG, and 1:8 for Play Group to ensure each child receives the personalized attention they deserve.
